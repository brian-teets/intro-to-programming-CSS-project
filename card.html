Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers make great family dogs!

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Temperament: Kind, friendly and confident
  • Lifespan: 10 - 12 years is the average
  • Bark Factor: Low to Moderate

Goldens generally have a gentle temperament. My wife and I have a Golden Retriever mix. He actually has a black coat from the other breed in him. He's such a sweet dog. He likes to snuggle on the couch. And, when we go hiking, he loves to lay down in cold streams.
